Move time

How long (ms) the device takes to reach the position you pick. Lower = snappier, more responsive tracking. Higher = smoother, gentler motion. It's also the beat of the Automate stroke.

− Slower / + Faster

Nudge move time in 40 ms steps without aiming at the slider. Slower raises the move time, Faster lowers it. Takes effect immediately, even mid-stroke.

Position slider

Drag it and the device travels to the same position — 0% is the bottom of the stroke, 100% the top. While Automate is on, this sets the peak of the stroke instead.

Automate

Continuously strokes between the bottom and your selected peak. Change the peak or the move time while it's running and it adapts live. Turning it on switches Bounce off.

Stop

Halts automation and brings the device back to rest at the bottom.

Bounce

When on, the device returns to the bottom after you release the slider — up to the selected position, then straight back down. Turning it on switches Automate off.

Range limit

Confines all motion to a window (e.g. 20–80%). The slider's full travel is remapped into it, so you keep full resolution over a shorter stroke.
